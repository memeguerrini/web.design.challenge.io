Max Temperature

There is a strong correlation between temperature and a latitude. This plot demonstrates temperature change depending on the latitude distance away from the equator. Temperature decreases directly with an increase in a latitude distance away from the zero-degree latitude. However, some inconsistency can be observed between Northern and Southern Hemispheres. As can be seen on the plot above, Northern Hemisphere tends to have a broader temperature range than the Southern Hemisphere. It is probable that temperature does not depend on a latitude alone and there are might be some other factors that affect temperature as well.

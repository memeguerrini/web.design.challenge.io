Humidity

The given plot demonstrates a very low correlation between latitude and humidity. In multiple instances, the same latitude can have humidity levels ranging from 20% to 100%, which indicates that humidity levels are affected by factors other than latitude. However, high levels of humidity, 60% - 100%, can be observed on the Southern Hemisphere very close to the equator. It is possible that humidity levels do relate to a latitude, but only in a close proximity to the zero-degree latitude.
